dear mika;
i have wanted to write u a (digital) letter for a long time,
and i guess now i finally am.

i am not sure i identify as an artist. i talked to a friend about it today, and they said "it sounds like u need to expand ur notion of artist."
maybe that is true.
but, maybe i dont need to identify as an artist (besides-
identifying as an artist seems counter intuitive; art is everything; art is experiential; art is collective.)

recently, i realized:

i want my art to nourish
i want to build a future
(that i believe in)

so, what does this look like?

i am still figuring it out, but it means, so far:

  • i want to make more collective art.
  • i want to make media that challenges capitalism and breeds a kinder future.
  • i want to make things that make people feel like magic is real.
